## Interview question of the week

This week’s question:\
**Given a number, sum every second digit in that number.**

Example:

```
> sumEveryOther(548915381)
> 26 // 4+9+5+8

> sumEveryOther(10)
> 0 

> sumEveryOther(1010.11)
> 1 // 0+0+1
```
