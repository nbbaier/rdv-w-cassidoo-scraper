 ## Interview question of the week

This week's question:\
**Given a direction and a number of columns, write a function that outputs an arrow of asterisks (see the pattern in the examples below)!**

Example:

```
$ printArrow('right', 3)
Output:
*
 *
  *
 *
*

$ printArrow('left', 5)
Output:
    *
   *
  *
 *
*
 *
  *
   *
    *
```
