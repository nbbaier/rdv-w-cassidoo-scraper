## Interview question of the week

**Let's say you have some special bags that can only hold up to 3kgs of weight. Given an array of integers (where each integer is between 1 and 3) that represent weights, determine how many bags you need to carry all of them.**

specialBag(\[2,2,2,1,3,1])\
\> 4 bags

\> specialBag(\[1,1,1])\
\> 1 bag
