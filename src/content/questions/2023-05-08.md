## Interview question of the week

This week's question:\
**Sum the odd-square numbers less than a given integer `n`.**

Example:

```
> oddSquareSum(1)
> 0

> oddSquareSum(2)
> 1

> oddSquareSum(9)
> 1

> oddSquareSum(10)
> 10

> oddSquareSum(44)
> 35
```
