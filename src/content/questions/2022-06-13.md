 ## Interview question of the week

This week's question:\
**Create a loooong teeeext generator that takes in a string and an integer `n`, and multiplies the vowels in the string by `n`.**

Example:

```
$ longText('hello world', 3)
$ 'heeellooo wooorld'

$ longText('lol', 10)
$ 'looooooooool'
```
