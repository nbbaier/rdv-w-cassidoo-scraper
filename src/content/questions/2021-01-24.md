 ## Interview question of the week

This week's question:\
**Given an n x n array, rotate it 90 degrees without making a new array.**

Example:

```
$ rotate90([[1,2,3],[4,5,6],[7,8,9]])
$ [[7,4,1],[8,5,2],[9,6,3]]
```
